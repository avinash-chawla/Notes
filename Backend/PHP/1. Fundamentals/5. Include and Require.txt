1. Include and Require

--> ninjas.php

<?php 
   $ninjas = ['shaun', 'ryu', 'yoshi'];
   echo $ninjas[1] . '<br />';
?>

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--> index.php

<?php 
    echo 'Start of line <br />';
    include('ninjas.php');
    echo 'End of line';
?>

<?php 
    echo 'Start of line <br />';
    require('ninjas.php');
    echo 'End of line';
?>

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--> Difference between them

--> include will not stop the execution of later code 
--> require will stop the execution of later code

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--> Advantage

--> They are partial views
--> You can use it at multiple pages and change at only place
